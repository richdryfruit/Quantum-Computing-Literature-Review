Abstract:
The goal of this paper’s thorough literature review on the various applications of quantum computing
is to analyze and summarize the various new developments in multiple interdisciplinary fields. This review aims to
delve into the theoretical aspects of quantum computing, such as superposition, entanglement, qubit manipulation,
and other fundamental elements, to develop a deep understanding of its applications. This paper examines various
research papers and publications to create a review that uncovers their uses in real-world applications. 6G internet,
algorithmic optimization, cryptocurrencies, semiconductor engineering, and transportation systems are a few to
name. The review also identifies the challenges faced in these fields and what the future holds, highlighting
the technological gaps and the upcoming developments to fill the void. Ultimately, this review provides a deep
understanding of the current standpoint of quantum computing and sheds light on the most promising future
directions.
